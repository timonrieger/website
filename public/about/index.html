Reading#

I mainly read older books via my Kindle according to the motto: The older the problem, the older the solution.

As for shorter reads, I read random personal blogs and a mix of programming, lifestyle and subreddit feeds via my RSS reader.

You can find an aggregated list of my current reads on my reads page.

Although I generally don’t consume visual content such as movies, entertainment, news programs and (even) sports (somehow I don’t miss it), I do enjoy watching some channels on Freetube (local youtube client).

Music#

For me, music is one of the most beautiful things in the world. I love listening to music at work and during sports, but never when I’m outside and wherever possible without headphones. Firstly, I don’t like the passive, disinterested, isolated posture of wearing headphones and secondly, they just never fit me.

Next to listening, I play the piano and occasionally have AI mix a few house tracks.

My current favorites are:

  • Gladiator soundtrack esp. Earth, The Battle, Honor Him and Now We Are Free (instantly hooked after watching Gladiator II)
  • 2s n 3s (remix of Rather Be which I really like as a warmup song in the gym)
  • In Memoriam by the young russian artist Kirill Richter
